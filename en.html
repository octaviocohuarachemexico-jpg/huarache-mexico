What Are Huaraches?

Huaraches are traditional Mexican leather sandals known for their breathability, durability, and comfort. With every step, they mold to your feet and become uniquely yours.

Enjoy the warmth and character of true handcrafted footwear, woven carefully by skilled artisans.

*Because each pair is handmade, slight variations in size and color may occur.

Our Story

Huarache Mexico began with a desire to introduce the beauty of authentic Mexican huaraches to Japan and beyond.

Rather than mass production, we focus on the value of carefully handcrafted items made one pair at a time.

We hope you can experience real Mexican culture through your footwear, wherever you are in the world.

Each pair carries the skills, traditions, and stories of the artisans who made them.

From across the ocean, directly to your doorstep.
